About Us

Food for All is a non-profit organization that aims to end hunger and reduce food waste by redistributing excess food from restaurants, grocery stores, and other food establishments to people in need.

Our mission is to create a sustainable food system that benefits everyone by reducing food waste and providing healthy and nutritious food to people in need.

We work with local food establishments and volunteers to collect, sort, and distribute food to homeless shelters, food banks, and other organizations that serve people in need.

Food for All was founded in 2015 by a group of food industry professionals who wanted to make a difference in their community. Since then, we have grown to serve over 10,000 people every month and have diverted millions of pounds of food from landfills.
